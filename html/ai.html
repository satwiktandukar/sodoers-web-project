Introduction

Artificial intelligence is the ability of a computer or machine to think and learn the way we call intelligence. It is the simulations of human activity by machines or computer. Artificial intelligence enables the system or program in learning, reasoning, and self-correction as required. AI is the work done by machines or computers as humans do.
