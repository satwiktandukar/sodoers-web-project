Web Design




Introduction

Web design refers to the design of websites that are displayed on the internet. It usually refers to the user experience aspects of website development rather than software development. Web design used to be focused on designing websites for desktop browsers; however, since the mid-2010s, design for mobile and tablet browsers has become ever-increasingly important.

A web designer works on the appearance, layout, and, in some cases, content of a website. Appearance, for instance, relates to the colors, font, and images used. Layout refers to how information is structured and categorized. A good web design is easy to use, aesthetically pleasing, and suits the user group and brand of the website. Many webpages are designed with a focus on simplicity, so that no extraneous information and functionality that might distract or confuse users appears. As the keystone of a web designerâ€™s output is a site that wins and fosters the trust of the target audience, removing as many potential points of user frustration as possible is a critical consideration.




Why we need Web Designing?

Web design is important because it impacts how your audience perceives your brand. The impression you make on them can either get them to remain on your page and learn about your business or leave your page and turn to a competitor. A good web design helps you keep your leads on your page.

Scope in context of Nepal

If you notice the latest trends in web designs of Nepal. You can analyze many people are opting for responsive design. This way, they don't have to make two different versions of their website for desktop and small screen devices. This is one of the major trends in web design. There has been a new trend in a mobile-first design approach. As these types of trends continue to emerge, there will be a surge in demand for web designers. The recent Information revolt has influenced the organizations, the general public, governments, social institution, and other sectors to advance from the offline world to the online world. Organizations wanting to have upper hand on their competitors are always looking to improve their presence offline or online. And in this journey to enhance their brand image - web designers play an essential role. Web designers have a responsibility to increase user retention in the website using various science-backed methodologies. Like the color of the buttons, the menu interaction, the support for various age groups, etc.
